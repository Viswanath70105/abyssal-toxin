# Chapter Outlines: Abyssal Toxin

This document outlines the narrative beats, environmental hazards, and key revelations across the 15 chapters of *Abyssal Toxin*. 

---

## Act 1: The Breach (Chapters 1-5)
*Theme: Confusion and Isolation. The station is falling apart, but the true nature of the threat is hidden.*

### Chapter 1: The Groan
*   **Narrative Beat:** The player (a junior investigator/auditor sent by Corporate) arrives at Nereus Station. The atmosphere is tense. The drill team has been dark for 12 hours.
*   **Station State:** Mostly intact, but power fluctuations are common.
*   **Revelation:** You find the first trace of the Abyssal Toxin (glowing mud) in the airlock, completely ignored by the stressed crew.

### Chapter 2: The Red Sector
*   **Narrative Beat:** The cavern breaches. An unidentified hostile mass floods the lower decks. The quarantine protocol slams down.
*   **Station State:** Lower Maintenance completely floods.
*   **Key Event:** Miller (Chief Mechanic) sacrifices himself in a jammed airlock so the player can escape. This sets the emotional stakes.
*   **Revelation:** This isn't a cave-in. Something is actively hunting the drill team.

### Chapter 3: Silent Alarms
*   **Narrative Beat:** The player investigates why the structural alarms didn't sound before the breach.
*   **Station State:** Oxygen rationing begins.
*   **Revelation:** You discover Miller’s sabotage and his sick daughter's medical bills, realizing your mentor was flawed.

### Chapter 4: The Hoarder & The Ghost
*   **Narrative Beat:** Food is missing. Paranoia sets in. You hear the first "ghostly" radio transmission from the deceased drill team.
*   **Station State:** Mess Hall lockdown.
*   **Revelation:** You confront Otto Finch and discover Maya, the stowaway child, surviving in the vents.

### Chapter 5: Blind Eyes
*   **Narrative Beat:** You try to access the security footage of the breach to see what came out of the cavern, but the servers are wiped.
*   **Station State:** Total blackout in the Security Hub.
*   **Revelation:** You deduce David wiped the servers to hide his smuggling operation, inadvertently blinding the station to the creature's initial movements.

## Act 2: The Rot (Chapters 6-10)
*Theme: Paranoia and Infection. The organism is inside the station, inside the vents, and inside the crew's minds.*

### Chapter 6: Muffled Screams
*   **Narrative Beat:** The organism starts using Miller’s voice over the comms to taunt the player. The player’s Infection UI begins at 10%.
*   **Station State:** Comms Tower is isolated.
*   **Revelation:** Chloe deleted the original distress call. She breaks down under the psychological pressure of the organism imitating the dead.

### Chapter 7: Bad Medicine
*   **Narrative Beat:** The crew starts showing physical signs of sickness. Dr. Aris locks down the Medbay.
*   **Station State:** Medbay becomes a hazard zone due to toxic spores.
*   **Revelation:** You confront Dr. Aris about the mislabeled vials and her hubris in trying to cure it alone without Corporate's help.

### Chapter 8: The Xenobiologist's Secret
*   **Narrative Beat:** Swarms of mutated rats attack the life support systems. The organism is breeding in the biology lab.
*   **Station State:** Ventilation system compromised.
*   **Revelation:** Victor deliberately infected the rats to find a cure for his dementia. His mind is nearly gone now.

### Chapter 9: The Pressure Drops
*   **Narrative Beat:** The station is groaning loudly. Another sector is about to collapse. The player must find out who broke the drill in the first place.
*   **Station State:** Engineering Bay is heavily damaged and structurally unsound.
*   **Revelation:** Arthur confesses to sabotaging the drill due to his PTSD, revealing he caused the breach while trying to protect them.

### Chapter 10: The Assimilation
*   **Narrative Beat:** The organism attacks in force, breaking into the safe zones. The player must make crucial choices about who to save based on previous alliances.
*   **Station State:** 50% of the station is now uninhabitable.
*   **Revelation:** The organism isn't just killing; it's absorbing memories and knowledge. It knows the station's layout better than the crew does.

---

## Act 3: The Scuttle (Chapters 11-15)
*Theme: Acceptance and Sacrifice. The truth is laid bare, and the final judgment must be made.*

### Chapter 11: Dark Water
*   **Narrative Beat:** The player is forced to swim through a flooded sector (Lower Maintenance) to restore auxiliary power. 
*   **Station State:** High Infection risk. UI glitches severely.
*   **Revelation:** You find Miller's body, fully assimilated. You must fight the husk of your mentor.

### Chapter 12: The Stowaway's Trail
*   **Narrative Beat:** Maya goes missing. The player tracks her through the darkest parts of the station.
*   **Station State:** Pitch black, bioluminescent spores provide the only light.
*   **Revelation:** Maya was the one who tracked the dormant spores into the vents while looking for her brother. The outbreak was a tragic accident.

### Chapter 13: Corporate Silence
*   **Narrative Beat:** Auxiliary power restored, the player manages to crack Gideon's private terminal.
*   **Station State:** Command Center accessible.
*   **Revelation:** You read Gideon's unsent transmission. Corporate isn't sending a rescue team. They never were. 

### Chapter 14: The Failsafe
*   **Narrative Beat:** The station begins an automated self-destruct sequence. Alarms blare. The ocean pressure starts crushing the upper decks.
*   **Station State:** Total catastrophic failure. Time limit engaged.
*   **Revelation:** You find the scuttling logs. It wasn't automated. Gideon initiated it manually. 

### Chapter 15: The Director's Cut
*   **Narrative Beat:** The climax. You confront Gideon Stroud at the only working escape pod. The organism is battering down the reinforced doors.
*   **Station State:** Only the Escape Bay remains.
*   **Revelation:** The final choice. Gideon explains his martyrdom—bury the infection to save Earth. The player must decide whether to accept his logic and die (Abyssal Grave), fight him to escape (Surface Tension), or succumb to the infection (Patient Zero).
