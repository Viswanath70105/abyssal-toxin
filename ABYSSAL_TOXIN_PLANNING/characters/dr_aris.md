# Dr. Aris (Lead Medical Officer)

## The Crime
Forged medical reports and covered up the initial signs of the mutagen outbreak among the crew.

## The Shame
She is a brilliant doctor who was hamstrung by corporate greed. Months ago, she noticed the crew developing early symptoms (metallic taste, tremors). She requested specialized medicine from Corporate, but they denied it to cut costs. 
Desperate, she forged the crew's health reports so Corporate wouldn't replace them or cut their hazard pay. She believed she could synthesize a cure herself using the station's limited lab equipment before anyone noticed. She worked 20-hour shifts in secret, slowly exhausting herself, but the mutagen mutated too fast. Her hubris and desperation to save everyone resulted in saving no one.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player suspects her of being a corporate spy covering up the outbreak. But when they find her secret logs, they see thousands of failed chemical synthesis attempts and audio recordings of her crying out of pure exhaustion, begging for more time to cure Miller.
