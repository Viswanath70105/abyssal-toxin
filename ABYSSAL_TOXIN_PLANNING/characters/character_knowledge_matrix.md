# Character Knowledge Matrix

| Character | What they know | What they think | What they lie about |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gideon Stroud** | The true nature of the mutagen, Protocol Omega's initiation, and that the station and crew are doomed. | He is a martyr doing the right thing. Sacrificing 40 innocent lives is necessary to save 8 billion on Earth. | Technically never lies, but withholds the truth about Protocol Omega and the station's scuttling until confronted directly. |
| **Dr. Aris** | The crew is infected with a highly adaptive Class-9 biological contaminant, her requisition was denied, and her cures are failing. | She is smart enough to beat the mutagen with local supplies and keep everyone from being burned by a corporate incineration squad. | The true state of the crew's health, falsely diagnosing severe mutagen infections as "Severe Vitamin D Deficiency." |
| **Miller** | The lower bulkheads were failing, the alarm system was physically bypassed, and his actions caused the cavern breach. | If they push through, he can get his hazard bonus to pay for his daughter's rare pediatric treatment. | The structural integrity of the station, claiming it's safe and ignoring warning signs. |
| **Arthur** | He manually overrode the drill's torque limiters, directly causing the catastrophic plunge into the abyssal cavern. | Breaking the drill would just cause a week of downtime, forcing them to rest and preventing a repeat of his past trauma. | The drill telemetry and failure, claiming the data is corrupted and the cavern breach was an accident. |
| **David** | He wiped the server logs, scrubbed camera feeds, and has a terminal illness with only six months left. | His petty theft of abyssal quartz is harmless and necessary to provide for his family after he dies. | The missing security footage over the last 48 hours, likely blaming system glitches. |
| **Chloe** | The drill team was slaughtered, what their horrific distress call sounded like, and that they died waiting for her. | She is a coward who deserves to suffer, constantly listening to the 'ghost' audio file to punish herself. | The fate of the drill team and the incoming emergency channel, claiming it was just static. |
| **Victor** | The mutagen can regenerate dead cellular tissue, he released infected subjects into vents, and his mind is deteriorating. | If he can synthesize the mutagen correctly, he can cure his early-onset dementia. | The containment of the organism and his unauthorized experiments in the ventilation shafts. |
| **Otto Finch** | Maya is on board, where she is hiding, and that she unknowingly tracked spores into the vents. | He must protect Maya at all costs and can hide her until they leave, starving himself for her. | Inventory discrepancies, constantly claiming the station is simply out of supplies or shipments were short. |
| **Maya** | She explored the deep-sea airlocks/lower decks and is looking for her brother. | Her brother is still alive somewhere on the station, and Otto is a "nice cook." | Doesn't actively lie, but hides her presence and movements from the rest of the crew. |
