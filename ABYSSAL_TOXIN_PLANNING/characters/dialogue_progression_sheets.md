# Dialogue & Emotional Progression Sheets

## Gideon Stroud - Station Director
* **State 1: Stoic & Authoritative** - Commanding respect, maintaining order, and giving clear directives to the crew.
* **State 2: Dismissive & Focused** - Ignoring minor complaints, focused entirely on the bigger picture of the station's purpose.
* **State 3: Resigned & Cold** - As the station falls apart, maintaining the lockdown and accepting the inevitable destruction.
* **State 4: Truthful & Unapologetic** - When confronted with evidence of Protocol Omega, admitting his role without hesitation or remorse.
* **State 5: Martyred & Resolute** - Fully accepting his fate, holding firm that his horrific sacrifice of the crew was necessary to save Earth.

## Dr. Aris - Lead Medical Officer
* **State 1: Professional & Rushed** - Busy with medical duties, dismissing minor concerns as typical deep-sea fatigue.
* **State 2: Anxious & Evasive** - When questioned about missing supplies or strange, mutating blood samples.
* **State 3: Defensive & Rationalizing** - Justifying her actions as "treating the symptoms" to avoid panic and corporate intervention.
* **State 4: Shattered & Desperate** - When her hubris is exposed, her medical requisition is found denied, and her cures fail.
* **State 5: Cooperative & Fatalistic** - Helping the player understand the organism's biology, knowing full well it is too late to save themselves.

## Miller - Chief Mechanic
* **State 1: Friendly & Mentoring** - Welcoming the player, showing them the ropes, acting as the emotional anchor.
* **State 2: Stressed & Deflecting** - When structural issues, failing bulkheads, and silenced alarms are brought up.
* **State 3: Desperate & Pleading** - Trying frantically to fix the catastrophic damage his sabotage caused to the station.
* **State 4: Guilty & Sacrificial** - Right before his death, realizing his desperate gamble for his daughter doomed everyone.
* **State 5: Distorted & Taunting (Organism Mimicry)** - The organism using his voice over the comms to manipulate, lure, and taunt the player.

## Arthur - The Engineer
* **State 1: Grumpy & Avoidant** - Complaining about corporate overreach, avoiding work, wanting to be left alone.
* **State 2: Paranoid & Agitated** - Triggered by alarms and station groans, his severe PTSD from a past incident surfacing.
* **State 3: Cornered & Angry** - When confronted with the drill telemetry showing his manual override.
* **State 4: Hollow & Ashamed** - Admitting his sabotage was a misguided attempt to force corporate to give them downtime.
* **State 5: Heroic & Redeemed / Broken (Conditional)** - If forgiven: sacrifices himself to vent the reactor. If falsely accused: suffers a mental breakdown and becomes infected.

## David - The Security Officer
* **State 1: Authoritative & Suspicious** - Acting as the station's enforcer, monitoring movements closely.
* **State 2: Secretive & Defensive** - When asked about the blind spots in cameras or the wiped surveillance footage.
* **State 3: Cornered & Hostile** - When confronted with the server wipe audit linking to his credentials.
* **State 4: Broken & Resigned** - Revealing his stage 4 terminal diagnosis and his petty smuggling motive for his family.
* **State 5: Helpful & Remorseful** - Giving access codes and security help to the player to make amends before he dies.

## Chloe - The Comms Technician
* **State 1: Jumpy & Paranoid** - Constantly looking over her shoulder, terrified of her own equipment.
* **State 2: Denial & Defensive** - Insisting the incoming emergency channel logs just show static.
* **State 3: Hysterical & Cornered** - When the 'Ghost' audio file of the slaughtered drill team is found on her drive.
* **State 4: Crushed & Ashamed** - Admitting her paralyzing cowardice and the fact that she let them die.
* **State 5: Resolute & Brave** - If supported, overcoming her fear to help route power and broadcast the warning in the "New Abyss" ending.

## Victor - The Xenobiologist
* **State 1: Eccentric & Fascinated** - Obsessed with the organism's unique biology and cellular properties.
* **State 2: Evasive & Protective** - Hiding the altered ventilation schematics and his unauthorized access.
* **State 3: Confused & Vulnerable** - Early-onset dementia symptoms showing; losing time, forgetting names.
* **State 4: Desperate & Confessional** - Admitting his unauthorized testing was a desperate hope to regenerate his own failing neural pathways.
* **State 5: Enlightened & Doomed** - Accepting the organism's true nature as his mind completely fades, fascinated to the very end.

## Otto Finch - The Cook
* **State 1: Genial & Harmless** - Friendly demeanor, controlling the mess hall, claiming typical supply chain issues.
* **State 2: Nervous & Overprotective** - When the player investigates areas near the dry storage or questions inventory.
* **State 3: Cornered & Defensive** - When large quantities of missing rations and medkits are discovered.
* **State 4: Pleading & Ashamed** - Revealing Maya's existence and admitting he has been starving himself to keep her hidden and alive.
* **State 5: Loyal & Selfless** - Fully aligning with the player, determined to protect the child at all costs.

## Maya - The Stowaway
* **State 1: Hidden & Fearful** - Fleeting glimpses in the dark, running away from the player.
* **State 2: Cautious & Curious** - Observing the player and the crew from the ventilation shafts.
* **State 3: Cornered & Terrified** - When discovered in her makeshift hideout in dry storage.
* **State 4: Sorrowful & Innocent** - Revealing she was just looking for her dead brother and unknowingly spread the spores.
* **State 5: Trusting & Dependent** - Looking to the player and Otto for safety and survival as the station collapses.
