# Victor (The Xenobiologist)

## The Crime
Smuggled living samples of the abyssal mutagen into unauthorized ventilation shafts and crew quarters for testing.

## The Shame
Victor was diagnosed with early-onset dementia before the mission. His mind is slowly deteriorating. When he analyzed the organism, he discovered that the mutagen aggressively regenerates dead cellular tissue.
He wasn't trying to infect the crew; he was desperately trying to test the mutagen on the station's rat population in the vents, hoping to synthesize a cure for his own failing brain. He viewed the organism not as a monster, but as a miracle. His unauthorized testing allowed the spores to enter the ventilation system, accelerating the outbreak.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player suspects them of being a cultist who worships the organism. However, the player finds their audio logs, which start out eloquent and sharp, but slowly devolve into confused, stuttering entries as their dementia progresses. They were just a brilliant mind terrified of losing themselves, trying to use the abyss to hold onto their humanity.
