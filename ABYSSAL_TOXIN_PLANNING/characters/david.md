# David (The Security Officer)

## The Crime
Deleted surveillance footage of Sector 4 and the Mess Hall over the last 48 hours.

## The Shame
David is terminally ill. To afford a comfortable life for his family after he's gone, he had been systematically disabling cameras to siphon small amounts of rare earth minerals from the cargo bays to sell on the black market. 
When the organism first breached the station, it happened in the exact sectors he had blinded. Because he deleted the footage to hide his smuggling, no one saw the mutagen spreading until it was too late. He realizes that his petty theft blinded the station to an existential threat.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player accuses him of deleting the footage to cover up Miller's murder. But when the player breaks into his locker, they don't find corporate assassination orders. They find stolen chunks of raw ore, a terminal diagnosis from a surface doctor, and letters from his family thanking him for the extra money. He's just a dying man who made a fatal mistake.
