# Miller (Chief Mechanic)

## The Crime
Ignored critical structural warnings and remained silent about the station's failing infrastructure.

## The Shame
Miller knew the station's lower bulkheads were failing months before the drill breached the cavern. Protocol dictated an immediate evacuation, which would have meant the crew returning to the surface without their hazard pay. 
Miller's daughter on the surface has a rare, expensive illness. He needed the massive completion bonus from this final rotation to afford her surgery. He silenced the alarms and patched the bulkheads with duct tape, convincing himself it would hold for just a few more weeks. His silence allowed the drill to keep operating, which eventually breached the cavern and woke the organism. 

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player finds his physical photo. Hidden behind the picture is a stack of past-due medical bills from an orbital hospital. Miller wasn't negligent; he was a terrified father making a desperate gamble to save his child, a gamble that doomed his friends.
