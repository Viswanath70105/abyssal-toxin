# Chloe (The Comms Technician)

## The Crime
Intercepted and deleted a horrific distress call from the initial drill team before they went silent.

## The Shame
When the drill breached the cavern, Chloe was on duty. She heard the screams of the drill team as the organism attacked them. It was the most terrifying thing she had ever heard.
Protocol required her to log the distress call and immediately join the emergency response team to go down into the dark and retrieve the survivors. Paralyzed by pure, visceral cowardice, she deleted the log and claimed it was static. The drill team slowly died in the dark while waiting for a rescue she ensured would never come.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player assumes she deleted the comms because she is a corporate spy hiding Protocol Omega. But if the player gains her trust, they find her listening to the recovered audio file of the screams on a loop in her quarters. She is punishing herself. The guilt has completely broken her mind, and she is desperate for someone to punish her for her cowardice.
