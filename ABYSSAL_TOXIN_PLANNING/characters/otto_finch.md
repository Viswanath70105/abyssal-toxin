# Otto Finch (The Cook)

## The Crime
Systematically stealing and hoarding food rations and medkits, causing the station-wide shortages that heightened tensions.

## The Shame
Otto isn't hoarding the food for himself. During the initial descent, he discovered a stowaway hiding in one of the cargo pods. 
However, the stowaway isn't just a desperate refugee. She is the sister of one of the original drill team members. When Corporate claimed the drill team was "lost at sea," she didn't believe them, so she smuggled herself aboard Nereus Station to find proof of what really happened to her brother.
Knowing Corporate would space her if she was found, Otto hid her in the abandoned sub-decks and has been starving himself to keep her alive. 
Tragically, because she was secretly exploring the lower decks searching for her brother's remains, she unknowingly tracked dormant spores from the drill site back up into the station's ventilation system, accelerating the outbreak. His selfless kindness accidentally became the catalyst for the station's doom.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player must find indirect evidence first—missing inventory logs, a footprint that doesn't match standard-issue boots, and a smuggled necklace belonging to the deceased drill team member. The Cook will never freely confess. Only when the player pieces together the stowaway's identity and confronts him with the proof will he break down. The player doesn't find a villain hoarding food; they find an innocent girl crying over her dead brother's necklace, and a man who ruined everything trying to protect her.
