# Arthur (The Engineer)

## The Crime
Sabotaged the deep-sea drill and bypassed the safety regulators, directly causing the cavern breach.

## The Shame
Ten years ago, on a different rig, Arthur followed corporate protocol to the letter. His strict adherence to the rules caused a younger worker to get trapped and die in an airlock. The trauma haunted him.
On Nereus Station, when Corporate ordered them to drill into the unstable trench, the Engineer decided he wouldn't let another crew die for a quota. He secretly sabotaged the drill's telemetry to make it look like they hit bedrock, hoping Corporate would call off the dig and send them home safely. 
Instead, the sabotage caused the drill to violently misalign and plunge into the abyssal cavern, waking the organism.

## The Tragedy (Player Discovery)
The player suspects the Engineer of causing the catastrophe on purpose (perhaps working for a rival corp). Through finding his old journals and the sabotaged parts, they realize his sabotage was a misguided attempt to *protect* the crew. His attempt to save lives is exactly what ended them.
