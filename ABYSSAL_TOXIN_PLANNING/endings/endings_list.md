# Endings

This document outlines the 8 possible endings for *Abyssal Toxin* and the conditions required to unlock them.

## 1. The Whistleblower (Best Ending)
**Description:** You escape the station, expose the Corporation's conspiracy, and ensure the surviving crewmates are rescued.
**Conditions:** 
* Identify Gideon Stroud as the saboteur.
* Save multiple crewmates (high trust).
* Gather and preserve all major evidence (Omega Protocol, drill logs, video footage).
* Escape via the Bathysphere.

## 2. The Corporate Asset
**Description:** You take the deal, escape with Stroud, and get rich. The world never knows about the mutagen.
**Conditions:**
* Confront Stroud but agree to his payoff.
* Wipe the mainframe logs as requested.
* Escape via the Bathysphere with Stroud.

## 3. Abyssal Madness (Transformed)
**Description:** You succumb to the mutagen and embrace the deep, becoming part of the organism. You survive in a transformed state, hinting at a sequel.
**Conditions:**
* High Infection level.
* "Befriend" the organism by refusing to destroy the Xenobiologist's samples.
* Fail or refuse to board the Bathysphere.

## 4. Crush Depth (Worst Ending)
**Description:** Failure to escape. The station implodes, and everyone dies in the dark.
**Conditions:**
* Fail critical mechanical skill checks during the Chapter 13 escape sequence.
* Run out of time during the final evacuation.

## 5. The Sacrifice
**Description:** You stay behind to manually detonate the station’s reactor, destroying the organism for good while your crewmates escape.
**Conditions:**
* Gather all technical specs of the station.
* Maintain high Trust with the Engineer.
* Choose to stay behind during the final sequence to trigger the meltdown.

## 6. The Quarantine
**Description:** You escape, but are captured by Corporate recovery teams and imprisoned as a biohazard. The truth is suppressed, but you’re kept alive for study.
**Conditions:**
* Escape via the Bathysphere.
* High Infection level.
* Fail to gather sufficient evidence to expose the Corporation to the public (or fail to broadcast it).

## 7. New Abyss
**Description:** You broadcast the organism’s existence on an open channel before dying. A cult or rival corporation intercepts it. The station sinks, but the creature is now on the radar of other powers.
**Conditions:**
* Confront Stroud and sustain fatal injuries (or fail to reach the pod).
* Successfully route power to the Comms Relay and broadcast the data.
* Have high Trust with the Comms Technician.

## 8. Mutual Destruction
**Description:** You confront Stroud, but neither of you reaches the pod. Both fall into the water as the organism rises. The final shot is the Bathysphere launching empty, carrying only the black box data.
**Conditions:**
* Engage in a physical struggle with Stroud at the Bathysphere (Chapter 14).
* Refuse the deal, but fail the combat/struggle checks.
* The black box data must have been placed in the pod beforehand.
