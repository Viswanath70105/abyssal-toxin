# Dialogue Drafts

This document contains sample dialogue scripts demonstrating the tone, character deflection mechanics, and the psychological horror of the organism.

---

## Scene 1: Interrogating Arthur (The Engineer)
**Context:** The player has just found the *Drill Telemetry Logs* and Arthur’s *Therapy Transcripts*. They corner him in the Engineering Bay. The station is groaning under the water pressure.

**[PLAYER]** 
(Present Drill Telemetry) "The logs don't lie, Arthur. The drill didn't break. You manually overrode the torque limiters. You shattered the cavern wall."

**[ARTHUR]** 
*(Deflecting, sweating profusely)* "I—I don't know what you're looking at. The telemetry is corrupted. The water pressure fried the servers days ago. You can't trust those numbers."

**[PLAYER]** 
"The timestamp is from *before* the breach. You did it on purpose."

**[ARTHUR]** 
*(Anger flaring, stepping forward)* "You think I wanted this? Corporate was pushing us too hard! We were hitting seismic pockets every hour. I told Gideon, I told all of them, the rocks were groaning! But they wouldn't listen!"

**[PLAYER]** 
(Present Therapy Transcripts) "It wasn't just about this dig, was it? You were terrified. The collapse on Titan-4... you thought it was happening again."

**[ARTHUR]** 
*(Arthur freezes. The anger drains out of him, replaced by a hollow, trembling exhaustion. He slumps against a console.)*
"...I just wanted to buy us a week. A week of downtime. If the drill was broken, they'd have to stop. They'd have to let us rest. I didn't know the cavern wall was that thin. I didn't know what was behind it. God... I just wanted us to be safe."

*Arthur collapses to his knees. The player can now choose to Arrest him, Forgive him, or Leave him.*

## Scene 2: The Organism's Taunt (Miller's Voice)
**Context:** Miller died in Chapter 2. It’s now Chapter 7. The player’s infection level is at 45%. The UI text is beginning to occasionally glitch. The player is walking through a flooded, dark corridor in the Lower Maintenance sector.

**[UNKNOWN_SPEAKER]** 
*Static crackles over the short-range radio.*
"...kid? You there? It's Miller."

**[PLAYER]** 
"Miller? No... no, I saw you die in the airlock."

**[MILLER_VOICE]** 
"Yeah. It hurt, kid. It hurt a lot. The water was so cold. Why didn't you cycle the door? Why did you leave me?"

*(If Infection Level > 40%, the dialogue text begins to physically distort on the screen. Words like 'hurt' and 'leave me' tremble and duplicate.)*

**[PLAYER]** 
"You're not him. You're the infection. You're just imitating him."

**[MILLER_VOICE]** 
*(The voice shifts, losing its warm, gruff tone. It becomes wet, distorted, echoing from the ventilation shafts above rather than the radio.)*
"I aM eVeryOne nOw. I caN tAsTe hiS mEmoireS. hE was tHinking of hIs dAughter when hE dRowNed. sHe is gOing tO dIe alOne beCause yOu wEre tOo sLow."

**[PLAYER]** 
*(Dialogue Option 1)* "Shut up! Shut up!"
*(Dialogue Option 2 - LOCKED due to Infection)* [SYSTEM ERROR: MENTAL STRESS TOO HIGH]
*(Dialogue Option 3)* Run.

**[MILLER_VOICE]** 
"cOme dOwn tO thE bOiler rOom. wE cAn fIx the aLarms tOgether."

## Scene 3: Confronting Dr. Aris (Lead Medical)
**Context:** The player discovers the *Mislabeled Vials* in the cold storage. Dr. Aris is frantically typing at her terminal when the player enters.

**[PLAYER]** 
(Present Mislabeled Vials) "Vitamin D deficiency, Aris? Really? These blood samples are practically eating through the glass."

**[DR. ARIS]** 
*(Doesn't look away from her screen. Types faster.)* "I am in the middle of a critical synthesis, detective. If you don't mind, the adults are trying to save the station."

**[PLAYER]** 
"You knew about the infection days before the quarantine. You lied on the official reports."

**[DR. ARIS]** 
*(Stops typing. Slowly turns around. Her eyes are bloodshot, her lab coat stained.)* 
"Of course I lied! Do you know what Corporate would have done if I told them we had a Class-9 biological contaminant onboard? They wouldn't have sent medicine. They would have sent an incineration squad!"

**[PLAYER]** 
"You should have warned us. People are dead because you thought you could handle it alone."

**[DR. ARIS]** 
*(Scoffs, stepping closer, poking a finger into the player's chest.)* 
"I am the only person on this rig with a doctorate in deep-sea pathology. I *can* handle it. I was so close. The mutagen is a puzzle, and I was one sequence away from breaking it. If you hadn't started sticking your nose where it doesn't belong and triggering the lockdown, I would have cured everyone by Tuesday!"

**[PLAYER]** 
(Present Rejected Medical Requisition) "You didn't have the supplies. Corporate denied your request. You were out of time, and you refused to admit it."

**[DR. ARIS]** 
*(Her arrogant posture shatters. She looks at the requisition form, her hands trembling.)*
"...I couldn't just let them burn us. I thought... I thought I was smart enough to beat it with what we had. I thought I was better than this."
