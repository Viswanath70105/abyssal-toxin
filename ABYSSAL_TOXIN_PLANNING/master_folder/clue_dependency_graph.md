# Clue Dependency Graph

## The Foundational Layer (Chapters 1-3)
- **Start:** Chapter 2 - Miller's Death.
- **Clue:** `Bypassed Relay Switch` + `Overdue Medical Bills` (Found in Maintenance/Miller's Locker).
  - *Unlocks:* The realization that structural warnings were ignored on purpose. 
  - *Leads to:* Investigating the Security Hub for more logs.

- **Clue:** `Server Wipe Audit` (Found in Security Hub).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **David (Security Officer)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Medical Diagnosis (Terminal)` + `Hidden Stash of Abyssal Quartz`.
  - *Leads to:* David's innocence regarding the monster is proven. The missing camera feeds force the player to search physical locations, starting with the Mess Hall.

## The Infection Vector (Chapters 4-6)
- **Clue:** `Inventory Discrepancies` + `Makeshift Bed` (Found in Mess Hall/Dry Storage).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Otto Finch (The Cook)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Child's Drawing`.
  - *Leads to:* Meeting **Maya (The Stowaway)**.

- **Clue:** `Small Muddy Footprints` + `Discarded Child's Jacket` (Found near Maya's Hideout / Vents).
  - *Unlocks:* Understanding how the spores spread through the vents.
  - *Leads to:* Investigating who was studying the spores in the Biology Lab.

- **Clue:** `Altered Ventilation Schematics` (Found in Biology Lab).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Victor (Xenobiologist)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Neurological Scans` + `Research Journal`.
  - *Leads to:* Realizing the mutagen regenerates dead tissue, pointing toward Medical.

## The Cover-Up (Chapters 7-9)
- **Clue:** `Mislabeled Vials` (Found in Medbay).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Dr. Aris (Lead Medical Officer)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Rejected Medical Requisition` + `Personal Audio Log`.
  - *Leads to:* Realizing the outbreak was hidden from Corporate, forcing the player to check communications.

- **Clue:** `Comms Relay Log` (Found in Comms Tower) + `The 'Ghost' Audio File`.
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Chloe (Comms Tech)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Empty Prescription Bottles`.
  - *Leads to:* The revelation that the drill team called for help. Why did the drill team fail? -> Leads to Engineering.

## The Catalyst & The Climax (Chapters 10-12)
- **Clue:** `Drill Telemetry Data` (Found in Engineering Terminal).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Arthur (The Engineer)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Therapy Transcripts` + `Half-Burned Manifesto`.
  - *Leads to:* Confirming the breach was caused by manual sabotage.

- **Clue:** `Scuttling Protocol Auth Log` (Found in Command Center).
  - *Unlocks:* Confrontation with **Gideon Stroud (Station Director)**.
  - *Obtains:* `Unsent Transmission to Earth Defense` + `A Faded Family Photo`.
  - *Leads to:* The Final Accusation and the ultimate choice in Chapter 15.
