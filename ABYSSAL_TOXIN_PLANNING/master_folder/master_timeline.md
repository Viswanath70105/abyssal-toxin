# ABYSSAL TOXIN: Master Timeline

## Months Before the Incident
*   **Corporate Discovers Anomaly:** Corporate detects the Abyssal Organism and initiates a cover-up. They order Station Director Gideon Stroud to secure it at all costs, hiding the fact that it is an extinction-level mutagen.
*   **The Stowaway:** Maya sneaks aboard Nereus Station in a cargo pod to search for her brother, a member of the deep-sea drill team.
*   **The Cook's Secret:** Otto Finch discovers Maya. He begins secretly hoarding food rations and medkits in the Dry Storage Room to keep her alive, starving himself in the process.
*   **Arthur Notices Unstable Geology:** Arthur (Engineer) hears the rocks groaning and warns Gideon, but is ignored. Driven by PTSD, he manually overrides the drill's torque limiters to sabotage the telemetry, hoping to break the drill and force Corporate to grant downtime.
*   **The Spores Enter:** While searching the lower decks for her brother, Maya unknowingly steps in dormant mutagen spores and tracks them into the station's ventilation system.
*   **Victor Joins / Experiments:** Victor (Xenobiologist), suffering from early-onset dementia, realizes the mutagen regenerates dead tissue. He begins smuggling living samples into unauthorized ventilation shafts to test on station rats in a desperate search for a cure.

## Weeks Before the Incident
*   **The Cavern Breach:** Arthur's sabotage backfires. The drill misaligns and violently plunges into the unstable abyssal cavern, exposing the station directly to the organism.
*   **Miller Bypasses Warnings:** Miller (Chief Mechanic) notices the lower bulkheads are failing from the breach. Needing his hazard pay to save his dying daughter, he bypasses the relay switch in the Lower Maintenance shaft to silence the structural warnings.
*   **The Drill Team Disappears:** The initial drill team is attacked by the organism in the deep-sea cavern. They transmit a horrific distress call back to the station.
*   **Chloe Deletes Distress Call:** Chloe (Comms Technician) intercepts the drill team's screams. Paralyzed by terror, she deletes the log, claiming it was static, and abandons them to die.
*   **Security Officer Smuggles:** David (Security Officer), diagnosed with a terminal illness, begins disabling cameras to siphon abyssal quartz from the armory vents, preparing a nest egg for his family.

## Days Before the Incident
*   **Aris Notices Blood Samples:** Dr. Aris (Lead Medical Officer) detects the initial signs of the mutagen outbreak. Desperate to avoid Corporate incinerating the station, she forges the medical reports, labeling the mutations as "Severe Vitamin D Deficiency."
*   **Victor Releases Rats:** Victor's infected rats begin spreading the mutagen more rapidly through the ventilation system, carrying it beyond the quarantined zones.
*   **Blind Spots:** David deletes surveillance footage of Sector 4 and the Mess Hall to cover his smuggling tracks over the last 48 hours, inadvertently blinding the crew to the organism's spread.
*   **Gideon's Realization:** Gideon views the unredacted files on the mutagen. He realizes that if it reaches the surface, Earth is doomed.

## Day 0 - The Incident (Minute-by-Minute Breakdown)
*   **07:00** - Final crew shift begins. Unseen by cameras, the organism fully infiltrates the lower decks.
*   **07:30** - Dr. Aris realizes her synthetic cure is completely useless against the organism's rapid adaptation.
*   **07:45** - David performs his final server wipe of the camera feeds.
*   **07:55** - Miller enters the airlock / Lower Maintenance to perform a routine check on the patched bulkhead. The organism violently breaches the sector.
*   **08:00** - Miller is exposed to a massive dose of the mutagen and fatally attacked. The organism begins mimicking him.
*   **08:05** - Miller collapses and dies. His death triggers localized alarms that cannot be bypassed.
*   **08:10** - Station-wide panic ensues as the crew realizes the lower decks are compromised.
*   **08:15** - Gideon Stroud makes his terrible choice. Using his personal biometrics, he initiates Protocol Omega (the scuttling sequence) to sink the station and bury the organism forever.
*   **08:20** - Station-wide Lockdown is initiated. Doors seal, trapping the crew in various sectors. The player's journey begins.
