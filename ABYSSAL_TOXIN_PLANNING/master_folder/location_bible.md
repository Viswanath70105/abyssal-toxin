# ABYSSAL TOXIN: Location Bible

## 1. Engineering (Engineering Bay)
*   **Purpose:** Controls the drill, reactor telemetry, and station power grid.
*   **Mood:** Tense, industrial, oppressive, deafeningly loud.
*   **Evidence:** Drill Telemetry Data (showing manual override of torque limiters).
*   **NPCs present:** Arthur.
*   **Lighting:** Harsh yellow and red emergency strobes.
*   **Sounds:** Groaning metal, deep humming of failing generators, warning klaxons.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 3.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 11 (Reactor goes unstable).

## 2. Medbay
*   **Purpose:** Medical treatment, quarantine, and crew health monitoring.
*   **Mood:** Clinical, sterile but stained, deeply paranoid.
*   **Evidence:** Mislabeled Vials (Cold Storage), Rejected Medical Requisition (Terminal), Therapy Transcripts (Arthur's PTSD).
*   **NPCs present:** Dr. Aris.
*   **Lighting:** Cold fluorescent, occasionally flickering to darkness.
*   **Sounds:** Humming medical equipment, irregular beeping of life support, dripping fluids.
*   **Locked until:** Accessible from Start / Chapter 2.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 13 (Station collapse).

## 3. Lower Maintenance
*   **Purpose:** Access to bulkheads, relay switches, and station infrastructure.
*   **Mood:** Claustrophobic, damp, profoundly dangerous.
*   **Evidence:** Bypassed Relay Switch (Miller's sabotage).
*   **NPCs present:** None (Haunted by the organism mimicking Miller's voice).
*   **Lighting:** Pitch black, requires flashlight/flare.
*   **Sounds:** Rushing water, distant screeches, echoing wet footsteps.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 3.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 6 (Sector B floods forever, destroying undiscovered clues).

## 4. Mess Hall
*   **Purpose:** Crew dining and central gathering area; adjoins Dry Storage.
*   **Mood:** Eerily quiet, abandoned mundane, tense starvation.
*   **Evidence:** Inventory Discrepancies (Terminal), A Child's Drawing (Pantry), Makeshift Bed (Dry Storage).
*   **NPCs present:** Otto Finch (and secretly Maya).
*   **Lighting:** Dim overhead lights, humming weakly.
*   **Sounds:** Buzzing refrigerators, faint shuffling in the vents above.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 1.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 12 (Structural failure).

## 5. Security Hub
*   **Purpose:** Surveillance monitoring, armory access, and door control.
*   **Mood:** Authoritarian, blind, paranoid.
*   **Evidence:** Server Wipe Audit (David's credentials), Hidden Stash of Abyssal Quartz (Armory Vents).
*   **NPCs present:** David.
*   **Lighting:** Blue glow of monitors; many screens show static or "NO SIGNAL".
*   **Sounds:** White noise from dead feeds, frantic clicking of keyboards.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 4.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 12.

## 6. Biology Lab
*   **Purpose:** Research on marine life and the deep-sea anomaly.
*   **Mood:** Obsessive, chaotic, biological horror.
*   **Evidence:** Altered Ventilation Schematics, Neurological Scans (Victor's Desk), Research Journal on Cellular Regeneration.
*   **NPCs present:** Victor (locked inside quarantine).
*   **Lighting:** UV lights, bioluminescent green glow from infected samples.
*   **Sounds:** Scurrying rats in the walls, bubbling specimen tanks, erratic scratching.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 6 (Quarantine Access).
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 13.

## 7. Command Center
*   **Purpose:** Station oversight and direct communication with Corporate.
*   **Mood:** Imposing, isolated, fatalistic, silent.
*   **Evidence:** Scuttling Protocol Auth Log.
*   **NPCs present:** Gideon Stroud.
*   **Lighting:** Low lighting, bathed in the red alert glow of Protocol Omega.
*   **Sounds:** Automated countdown whispers, muffled immense ocean pressure groaning against the reinforced glass.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 9.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 14 (Final confrontation).

## 8. Comms Tower
*   **Purpose:** External communication and routing of distress signals.
*   **Mood:** Desolate, haunting, regret-filled.
*   **Evidence:** Comms Relay Log, The 'Ghost' Audio File (Chloe's Drive).
*   **NPCs present:** Chloe.
*   **Lighting:** Shadowy, erratic flashing from the comms panels.
*   **Sounds:** Snatches of radio static, whispered echoes, distorted screams bleeding through dead channels.
*   **Locked until:** Chapter 5.
*   **Destroyed when:** Chapter 12.
