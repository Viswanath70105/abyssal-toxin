# Items & Clues Design Document

This document outlines the **Indirect Evidence** the player must find to deduce the **Crime** and **Shame** of each character in *Abyssal Toxin*. In the game, there are no free confessions. The player must assemble these clues in their Detective Notebook to unlock confrontation dialogues. 

---

## 1. Gideon Stroud (Director)
**Crime:** Initiated the scuttling protocol to bury the station.
**Shame:** He knew the mutagen was apocalyptic and chose to sacrifice the crew to become a martyr and save Earth.

*   **Clue 1: Unsent Transmission to Earth Defense (Personal Terminal)**
    *   *Description:* A heavily encrypted, unsent draft. 
    *   *Content:* "The organism is highly adaptive and hostile. Standard containment has failed. I am executing the final failsafe. Do not send rescue. If this reaches the surface, humanity ends."
*   **Clue 2: Scuttling Protocol Auth Log (Command Center)**
    *   *Description:* A hard-copy printout of the station's final command execution.
    *   *Content:* Shows that the scuttling sequence wasn't triggered by an automated catastrophic failure, but via Gideon's personal biometrics.
*   **Clue 3: A Faded Family Photo (Gideon's Quarters)**
    *   *Description:* A picture of Gideon with his wife and child.
    *   *Content:* On the back, written in shaky handwriting: "I'm sorry. I have to keep you safe from this. Don't look for me."

## 2. Miller (Chief Mechanic)
**Crime:** Silenced the failing structural alarms.
**Shame:** Did it to get hazard pay to treat his terminally sick daughter.

*   **Clue 1: Bypassed Relay Switch (Lower Maintenance shaft)**
    *   *Description:* A physical piece of the station's alarm system, deliberately fried.
    *   *Content:* The sabotage is clean, professional. Only a master mechanic like Miller could have bypassed it without triggering a secondary alert.
*   **Clue 2: Overdue Medical Bills (Miller's Locker)**
    *   *Description:* Crumbled, tear-stained invoices from an Earth-side hospital.
    *   *Content:* Astronomical costs for a rare pediatric treatment. A final notice of care termination due to insufficient funds.
*   **Clue 3: Denied Leave Request (HR Terminal)**
    *   *Description:* A formal request to return to Earth, denied by Corporate.
    *   *Content:* Note attached: "We need you here, Miller. Complete the cycle, get the hazard bonus. It’s the only way."

## 3. Dr. Aris (Lead Medical)
**Crime:** Forged medical reports to hide the initial outbreak.
**Shame:** Hubris. She thought she could cure it in secret because Corporate refused to send medicine.

*   **Clue 1: Mislabeled Vials (Medbay Cold Storage)**
    *   *Description:* Blood samples hidden behind generic vaccines.
    *   *Content:* Labeled as "Severe Vitamin D Deficiency," but under a microscope, the cells are violently mutating.
*   **Clue 2: Rejected Medical Requisition (Medbay Terminal)**
    *   *Description:* An urgent request for broad-spectrum antivirals and quarantine gear.
    *   *Content:* Corporate response: "Denied. Too expensive. Treat with local supplies and keep the drill running."
*   **Clue 3: Personal Audio Log (Dr. Aris's PDA)**
    *   *Description:* A frantic, late-night recording.
    *   *Content:* "It's adapting faster than I can synthesize a counter-agent. If I report this now, they'll just seal the sector with us inside. I can fix this. I just need more time."

## 4. Arthur (The Engineer)
**Crime:** Sabotaged the drill, accidentally causing the cavern breach.
**Shame:** Traumatized by a past accident, he was trying to protect the crew from corporate greed pushing too deep.

*   **Clue 1: Drill Telemetry Data (Engineering Terminal)**
    *   *Description:* The raw logs from the day of the breach.
    *   *Content:* Shows a manual override of the drill's torque limiters. It wasn't a mechanical failure; someone forced the drill to shatter the cavern wall.
*   **Clue 2: Therapy Transcripts (Medbay Records)**
    *   *Description:* Mandatory psychological evaluations for Arthur.
    *   *Content:* Details severe PTSD from a previous mining collapse. "I can hear the rocks groaning again. They're pushing us too fast. Someone's going to die again."
*   **Clue 3: Half-Burned Manifesto (Incinerator Room)**
    *   *Description:* A charred piece of paper written by Arthur.
    *   *Content:* "Corporate won't listen. The core is unstable. I have to break the drill. A week of downtime is better than all of us dead."

## 5. David (Security Officer)
**Crime:** Deleted security footage during the initial outbreak.
**Shame:** Terminally ill, he was smuggling minerals to leave money for his family.

*   **Clue 1: Server Wipe Audit (Security Hub)**
    *   *Description:* A system log detailing deleted files.
    *   *Content:* Shows David's credentials were used to scrub all camera feeds in the lower decks at the exact time the infection breached the station.
*   **Clue 2: Hidden Stash of Abyssal Quartz (Armory Vents)**
    *   *Description:* Extremely valuable, unprocessed raw minerals.
    *   *Content:* Packaged for smuggling. This is what David didn't want the cameras to see.
*   **Clue 3: Medical Diagnosis (David's Quarters)**
    *   *Description:* A private doctor's note from Earth, hidden in a book.
    *   *Content:* Stage 4 terminal illness. "Six months left. Get your affairs in order."

## 6. Chloe (Comms Tech)
**Crime:** Deleted the distress call from the drill team.
**Shame:** Pure cowardice; she listens to their screams every night as punishment.

*   **Clue 1: The 'Ghost' Audio File (Chloe's Personal Data Drive)**
    *   *Description:* An encrypted audio file she keeps on her person.
    *   *Content:* The original, horrific distress call from the drill team being slaughtered by the organism. She saved it instead of broadcasting it.
*   **Clue 2: Comms Relay Log (Communications Tower)**
    *   *Description:* The station's outgoing transmission record.
    *   *Content:* Shows an incoming emergency channel was manually muted and purged from the general broadcast system.
*   **Clue 3: Empty Prescription Bottles (Chloe's Quarters)**
    *   *Description:* Numerous bottles of heavy sedatives and sleeping pills.
    *   *Content:* She hasn't slept naturally since the breach. She takes them to stop hearing the screams.

## 7. Victor (Xenobiologist)
**Crime:** Smuggled mutagen into the vents to test on rats.
**Shame:** Early-onset dementia. Desperate to cure his own failing brain.

*   **Clue 1: Altered Ventilation Schematics (Biology Lab)**
    *   *Description:* Blueprints of the station's air flow.
    *   *Content:* The vents leading from the lab to the lower decks are highlighted in red marker, mapping out where he released the infected test subjects.
*   **Clue 2: Neurological Scans (Victor's Desk)**
    *   *Description:* Brain scans with Victor's name on them.
    *   *Content:* Showing rapid, irreversible deterioration. Notes in the margin read: "Losing time. Forgetting names. Need a solution."
*   **Clue 3: Research Journal on Cellular Regeneration**
    *   *Description:* A notebook filled with frantic scribbles.
    *   *Content:* "The organism doesn't just infect; it repairs and reanimates dead tissue. If synthesized correctly, it could repair my neural pathways. It’s the only cure."

## 8. Otto Finch (The Cook)
**Crime:** Hoarding massive amounts of food.
**Shame:** Keeping Maya (the stowaway) alive in secret.

*   **Clue 1: Inventory Discrepancies (Mess Hall Terminal)**
    *   *Description:* The official ration manifest.
    *   *Content:* Large quantities of high-calorie, long-lasting rations are missing, far more than Otto could eat himself.
*   **Clue 2: A Child's Drawing (Pantry Shelves)**
    *   *Description:* A crayon drawing hidden behind a sack of flour.
    *   *Content:* Depicts a scary monster in the dark, and a "nice cook" giving out food.
*   **Clue 3: Makeshift Bed (Dry Storage Room)**
    *   *Description:* A nest made of blankets and old coats.
    *   *Content:* It's way too small for a grown man. Someone else has been living here.

## 9. Maya (The Stowaway)
**Crime:** Tracked dormant spores into the ventilation system.
**Shame:** She was searching the lower decks looking for her dead brother.

*   **Clue 1: Small Muddy Footprints (Ventilation Shafts)**
    *   *Description:* Tracks leading from the deep-sea airlocks into the station's core.
    *   *Content:* The boots are far too small to belong to any of the adult crew members.
*   **Clue 2: Circled Crew Manifest (Maya's Hideout)**
    *   *Description:* A crumpled piece of paper with the crew names.
    *   *Content:* A name from the drill team is heavily circled, with the word "WHERE?" written next to it. (Later found to be marked 'Deceased').
*   **Clue 3: Discarded Child's Jacket (Near the Breach Site)**
    *   *Description:* A small jacket covered in abyssal mud.
    *   *Content:* The hem of the jacket is glowing faintly with the bioluminescence of the mutagen spores.
